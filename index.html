About

Our team consists of a group of friends with a shared passion for the possibilities offered by digital revolution. We think that virtual worlds, blockchain, tokenized art and VR have the potential to enable yet unseen manifestations of imagination, while at the same time being never more accessible to anyone willing to explore and build using these technologies. So we decided to unite our existing skills in 3D modeling, programming, animation, architecture and design to create an agency that can offer the world-building services to anyone.

Decentraland, CryptoVoxels, Somnium Space and VRChat are virtual worlds we believe have the most potential to become the building blocks of proper metaverse.

Besides design and modeling, we also offer adding interactivity to the worlds and items we create. Tools we like to use are Blender, 3ds Max, Unity, VS Code, Oculus. Our work process is directed by agile development methods, meaning that communication with the client and early detection of changes is key in getting the project done stress-free in a timely manner.
